Vision & Mission

Mission :

Our world today is witnessing disastrous wars and ruthless conflicts, hiring mercenaries to kill, not only military rivals but also innocent civilians including men, women and children. These wars are not only between nations but also within countries and between tribes or religious sects. What is the answer to all this dispute and misery?

Sustainable peace and truthful unity are based on upholding absolute moral standards and core principles shared by all humanity throughout the ages.

An alliance of moral forces is now urgently needed to uphold the sanctity of human life, liberate man and restore unity and sanity to this troubled world. We need an alliance that demonstrates ethical leadership, courage, faith, wisdom and sacrifice.
As former US president Jimmy Carter put it “We need to wage peace as others wage war.”

What alliance can save humankind, restore unity among people, and lead the way to firm and lasting peace?

“O believers! Stand firm for God and bear true testimony. Do not let the hatred of a people lead you to injustice. Be just! That is closer to righteousness. And be mindful of God. Surely God is All-Aware of what you do.” Meaning of verse 8 chapter (5) of the holy Quran.
