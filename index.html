Introduction

Our world today is witnessing disastrous wars and ruthless conflicts, hiring mercenaries to kill, not only military rivals but also innocent civilians including men, women and children. These wars are not only between nations but also within countries and between tribes or religious sects. What is the answer to all this dispute and misery? Sustainable peace and truthful unity are based on upholding absolute moral standards and core principles shared by all humanity throughout the ages.

An alliance of moral forces is now urgently needed to uphold the sanctity of human life, liberate man and restore unity and sanity to this troubled world. We need an alliance that demonstrates ethical leadership, courage, faith, wisdom and sacrifice.

About us

We are a team of concerned human beings worried about the disastrous situation in the world today and longing for an end to suffering promoting the sanctity of human life and restoring sustainable peace through the revival and adherence to shred human values and moral principles. We believe that if we put our heads together, hearts together and hands together we can achieve progress and fulfill our noble goals.

Our Goal

Our goal is to contribute to rescuing humanity from a disastrous destiny, to find the light at the end of the dark tunnel we are all going through bord Noah;s Ark and reach a safe shore by God’s Grace and Guidance..

Our Team

Our team is multi-generational multi-talented and interfaith. Most of us are affiliated with the Egyptian Moral Rearmament Association (EMRA) which has been established and registered in the Egyptian Ministry of Social Welfare since 1988 and  is a member of Initiatives of change international association (IofC) / MRA) registered in the UN since 2002. Synergy   implies that the behavior of the whole is more than the sum of the parts.
